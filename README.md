<h3>Projects I'm working on</h3>

- [Partitions](https://github.com/LudovicAL/Partitions) : A database of traditionnal sheet music
- [Folkset](https://github.com/LudovicAL/FolkSets) : An Android app that display and organizes sheet music
- [Compasses](https://github.com/LudovicAL/Compasses) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing 4 different compasses for video games
- [Mini Map 3d](https://github.com/LudovicAL/MiniMap3d) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing a minimap in three-dimensional space
- [Hyperbolic Shader](https://github.com/LudovicAL/HyperbolicShader) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing a shader with a hyperbolic-like effect
- [Mirrors](https://github.com/LudovicAL/Mirrors) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing two mirror-simulation-techniques
- [Non Euclidean](https://github.com/LudovicAL/NonEuclidean) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing seamless portals to simulate a non euclidean environment
- [Stencil Shader](https://github.com/LudovicAL/StencilShader) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing renderer-slicing using two techniques
- [Displacement Shader](https://github.com/LudovicAL/DisplacementShader) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing a shader capable of warping one's view
- [Unity Tilemap Space](https://github.com/LudovicAL/UnityTilemapSpace) : A proof of concept 'minigame', showcasing two-story houses in a 2d tilemap environment
- [Out Of The Way](https://github.com/LudovicAL/OutOfTheWay) : A simple 4 players game where the goal is to jump on your opponent's head.
- [PacMan](https://github.com/LudovicAL/PacMan) : A Pacman remake
